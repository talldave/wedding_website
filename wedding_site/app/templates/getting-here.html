Getting Here


Planes


Although Santa Rosa is the closest airport, the best fares will be into San Francisco’s airport (SFO) or Oakland (OAK). Please remember this is Memorial Day weekend! The wedding is not until Sunday evening so hopefully this gives you some flexibility for travel dates.

Trains


We wish! The Sonoma Marin Area Rail Transit (SMART) is coming but not in time for our wedding.

Automobiles


The best option for getting around with no restrictions is to rent a car. If you don’t want to rent a car, there is a $35 shuttle that runs from SFO to Petaluma and a taxi from Petaluma to Westerbeke Ranch should run about the same.

Petaluma is about an hour north of San Francisco, 1.5 hours from SFO (with a little traffic).
Map: SFO to Petaluma

Westerbeke Ranch is in Sonoma, about a 20 min drive east from Petaluma.
