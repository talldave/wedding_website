Staying Here


Petaluma is our Wedding HQ for the weekend. Petaluma has four hotels and plenty of vacation homes for rent via VRBO and AirBnB. There is also an option to spend one night (Sunday May 25th) at the wedding venue, Westerbeke Ranch. David and Carolyn will be at the Metro Hotel on Friday and Saturday, and then the Cabins at Westerbeke Ranch for Sunday night.

Hotels


Sheraton Sonoma County Hotel

~$210/night

This is the most upscale of Petaluma's hotels, centrally located 3 minutes by car from downtown Petaluma, 20 minutes from Westerbeke Ranch, and with convenient freeway access. They have an outdoor swimming pool, lounge, restaurant, and bar.


Best Western Petaluma Inn

~$100/night

This hotel is 4 minutes by car from downtown Petaluma, 20 minutes from Westerbeke Ranch, and has very convenient freeway access. This hotel is the closest one to the Petaluma fairgrounds if you are taking the shuttle from SFO. They have an outdoor swimming pool and breakfast is included.


Quality Inn

~$170/night

The Quality Inn is a bit further north, 10 minutes by car from downtown Petaluma, 25 mins from Westerbeke Ranch, and very convenient freeway access. They also have an outside pool.



Metro Hotel and Cafe

~$130/night

At last check, the Airstream room and their offsite cottages were still available.



Cabins at Westerbeke Ranch

$100/Person

For Sunday Night only! Join us for a night at the cabins at Westerbeke Ranch. There are 17 cabins with various bed configurations. If this is an option you would like to explore, you must contact us to make the arrangements.
