Weather


The key to dressing in the San Francisco Bay area is layers! In San Francisco, once the sun sets, the temperature drops. Nights are warmer in the north bay (where we will be) but we still recommend bringing a sweater with you. The weather Memorial Day weekend could be "cool" (in the 60's) or "warm" (in the 80's) and there is a small chance it could rain. We recommend checking the weather a week or so before the wedding - the forecast should give you a good feel for the conditions by then.

Weather permitting, the ceremony and reception will be outside. Heat lamps will be used if the evening is expected to be on the cooler side.
